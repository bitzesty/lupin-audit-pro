About

Lupin Systems

AuditPro is an affiliate program by Lupin Systems.

Lupin Systems was established in 2010 to develop and market integrated business software solutions for occupational Health and safety management. Previously the two principals of Lupin Systems developed software that was used in a house as in house consulting tools for local, national and international organisations.

Lupin Asbestos Management was developed to completely manage hazardous building materials, for example asbestos, within buildings and structures; from real-time auditing through to 24/7 register access. This product ensure that organisations are compliant with current legislation. In addition they provide significant productivity gains through streamlined data capture and storage and the generation of documentation.

The team at Lupin Systems are experts in occupational Health & Safety legislation. They understand what is required to comply with these laws and ensure that their software keeps pace with changes to legislation.
