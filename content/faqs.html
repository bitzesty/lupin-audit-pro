FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I become an affiliate?

Lupin will process your application within 48 hours and issue you with a username and password. This will give you access to Lupin-Asbestos Management software and access to the AuditPro website.

How do I register my larger clients with AuditPro?

Send an email to pre-register your client. Lupin will bill them before the audit is conducted. We will send you an email to confirm receipt of payment and your earned commission.

How will AuditPro support me with my sales and marketing?

Once you are registered and accepted you will receive an AuditPro Affiliate kit. This will include all the information you need to give to your customers.

How do I get paid?

For larger commercial or government audits, the end client will pay Lupin based on the number of sites. Your commission will be paid to you by us.

Does it cost me anything to be an AuditPro affiliate?

As an AuditPro Affiliate, you can use Lupin Asbestos Management software for smaller commercial and domestic audits at a greatly reduced price.

For larger commercial or government audits, the client will pay Lupin directly.
